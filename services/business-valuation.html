What Our Business Valuation Process Covers

A business valuation from Spearpoint Advisors isn't just a dollar figure. It's a comprehensive analysis of your business across every dimension that affects what a buyer, lender, or successor would pay.

Current Market Value

What is your business worth today, based on your financial performance, industry multiples, and the specific characteristics of your company?

Value Drivers and Detractors

What is making your business more valuable — and what is quietly reducing what a buyer would pay? Owner dependency, customer concentration, inconsistent margins, leadership gaps.

Value Gap Analysis

What is the distance between where your business is valued today and where it could be with deliberate improvement? We map the gap and show you which changes would have the highest impact.

Industry Multiple Context

How does your business compare to industry benchmarks? What multiples are buyers paying for businesses like yours — and what separates top-quartile exits from median ones in your sector?

Value Acceleration Roadmap

Based on the valuation findings, we develop a prioritized roadmap for closing the value gap — connecting the business valuation work directly to the Prepare gate of our process.

Most Owners Don't Know What Their Business Is Actually Worth

That's not a criticism. Business owners spend their time running their companies — not analyzing them through the lens of a buyer, a lender, or a successor. The result is a significant gap between what owners believe their business is worth and what the market will actually pay.

That gap has real consequences. It affects how you think about growth investments, how you structure your personal finances, how you approach succession or sale conversations, and whether you're protected if one of the 5 Ds forces an exit you didn't plan for.

"The owners who get the best outcomes at exit aren't the ones who got lucky. They're the ones who understood what their business was worth — and spent years closing the gap."
