80% of those owners had no plan in place when it happened — despite 80% of their personal net worth being tied up in the business they just lost control of.

Think about what that means in practice:

* No exit plan means you take whatever the market offers — not what you're worth  
* No leadership depth means the business value walks out the door with you  
* No operational systems means a buyer sees risk, not opportunity  
* No succession structure means family conflict, legal exposure, and lost time

The 5 Ds don't announce themselves. The owners who are protected when they arrive are the ones who started preparing before they needed to.
